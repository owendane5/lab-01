The Biography

Starting right off at my education the highschool choices I had went as far as living in a small town outside of the city would get you. With that in mind I completed my high school diploma at Chelmsford Valley District Composite School (CVDCS).

After highschool I decided to take a year off to simultaneously take time to think of what I was gonna pursue as well as work full time to save up some money. During this time I worked at a machine shop. At this shop I was gonna agree to an apprentenship as I was doing great work there but, ultimitly I decided to move in to the programming field.

After I made this decision it wasn't long before I apllied to attend Georgian College for programmin and fulfill one of my many passions.
